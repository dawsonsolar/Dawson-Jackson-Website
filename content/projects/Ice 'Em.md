**Ice 'Em** is a game inspired by "Worms" built for **Winter Game Jam 2025**. The jam version was made with a team of three over a single weekend — I served as lead programmer.

After the jam I liked it enough to keep going, and I'm now developing it further on my own time. However this is mostly for experience purposes, and won't see any release anytime soon or ever.

## What I built

- Core gameplay loop and player controller in Unity/C#
- Enemy AI and tracking through/around obstacles
- Stats tracking and game state management
- Integrated all artist and designer assets into a cohesive build in the allotted game jam window.

## Current Status

Actively in development, Expected haitus May 2026. Working on expanding the level design with tilemaps, polishing the feel of the controls and Enemies, and adding new mechanics beyond the jam scope (items, thin ice).
