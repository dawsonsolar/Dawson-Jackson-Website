**Ice 'Em** is a turn-based game inspired by *Worms*, built for **Winter Game Jam 2025** over a single weekend with a team of three. I served as lead programmer.

After the jam I liked the concept enough to continue developing it solo.

## My Role

- **Player controller** — built the core movement and action system in Unity/C#, including turn management and input handling
- **Enemy AI** — implemented pathfinding and obstacle-aware tracking so enemies navigate around level geometry
- **Stats & game state** — designed the game state manager handling turns, win/loss conditions, and score tracking
- **Asset integration** — coordinated with artists and the designer to integrate all visual and audio assets within the jam window
- **Solo post-jam work** — continuing to expand level design with tilemaps, refining enemy behavior, and adding new mechanics (items, thin ice mechanics)

## Current Status

Actively in development. Expected hiatus May 2026 due to coursework. Intended as a personal learning project and not slated for public release.
